## The schedule is out!

We've released the [schedule](/events) for the inaugural NW IdolFest, set to take place NEXT WEEKEND, Nov 13-14!

Programming will kick off at 10:00 AM on Saturday and span two rooms, [Main Auditorium](/events/mainauditorium) and [Class A](/events/classa). In addition to some amazing panels and workshops, don't miss our Northern Lights Music Festival on Saturday night. 

We can't wait to see you there!
